The Problem We Solve

Our Planet Is Facing a Crisis It Cannot Ignore

Water bodies worldwide are choked with pollution, debris, and toxic runoff — while the air above our cities carries invisible threats: particulate matter, carbon monoxide, greenhouse gases. Manual intervention is slow, expensive, and inconsistent. Skycleanse was built to change that.

By deploying both an autonomous aquatic vessel and a sensor-equipped aerial drone, we address water and air pollution simultaneously — collecting real-time data and acting on it directly in the field.

Water Pollution

Rivers, lakes, and canals accumulate plastics, organic debris, and chemical runoff at an alarming rate.

Air Quality Crisis

PM2.5, carbon monoxide, and CO₂ levels in urban and agricultural areas cause lasting health damage.

Manual Monitoring Fails

Human-operated monitoring is infrequent, inconsistent, and unable to cover large geographic areas.

Agricultural Waste

Excess fertilizer use degrades soil and waterways. Precision deployment is needed to reduce waste.

Project 01 — Riverix

Semi-Autonomous Water Surface Robot

Riverix is an advanced Autonomous Surface Vehicle (ASV) built around an ESP32 microcontroller. It navigates polluted waterways — rivers, lakes, ponds, and canals — collecting real-time environmental data and autonomously deploying water treatment solutions. It operates in both manual RC and autonomous GPS waypoint modes, with live telemetry streamed to a web dashboard.

Project 02 — Sky Cleanse

Semi-Automated Environmental Drone

Sky Cleanse is an aerial drone that simultaneously monitors air quality, purifies polluted air through a MOXI filter system, and supports precision crop fertilization. Equipped with five specialized atmospheric sensors, it gathers real-time data on particulate matter, toxic gases, CO₂, temperature, and humidity — acting as both a monitor and a purifier.

Core Technology

Engineering Behind the Mission

Both platforms leverage complementary technologies that form a complete environmental intelligence and remediation ecosystem.

ESP32 Microcontroller

The brain of Riverix — handling GPS processing, PID navigation, sensor fusion, obstacle avoidance, telemetry, and pump control simultaneously via real-time multitasking.

GPS + IMU Navigation

Coordinate-based waypoint tracking with MPU6050 (accelerometer + gyroscope) and HMC5883L compass for heading accuracy. PID controller minimizes angular error continuously.

Multi-Sensor Arrays

Nine sensors across both platforms: pH, TDS, turbidity, temperature (water) + PMS7003, MQ-7, MQ-135, DHT-22, MH-Z19E (air) — delivering continuous environmental intelligence.

ESP-NOW Telemetry

Low-latency peer-to-peer wireless communication streams GPS coordinates, sensor readings, motor status, and obstacle data to the ground control station in real time.

Modular 3D-Printed Parts

Custom brackets, MOXI filter housings, and sensor mounts are 3D-printed for lightweight construction, precise fitment, and easy field replacement without specialist tools.

Web Ground Control Station

A live dashboard accessible via Wi-Fi shows real-time map positioning, sensor charts, waypoint management, pump control, and autonomous mission triggers for Riverix.
